 - You play as the paddle.
 - You can change the size of the paddle by 
choosing a difficulty when prompted at the 
beginning of the game.
 - You can move left and right using the 
arrow keys.
 - If the ball touches the danger zone 
underneath the paddle, then you lose a life.
 - You have 10 lives in total.
 - Break all the bricks in a level to 
move on.
 - There are 5 stages.
 - Try to get the highest score possible.
